BUDT748 INDUSTRY PRACTICUM
BUDT748 is the capstone course for Information Systems (IS) majors. Students gain hands-on experience by collaborating with real clients to analyze requirements, design solutions, and develop functional projects. The course emphasizes teamwork, project management, and applying both technical and business skills to solve real-world challenges. By the end of the semester, students present their deliverables to clients and faculty, demonstrating their ability to manage the full project lifecycle.
